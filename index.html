About Me

At the age of 7, I fell in love with investigating how technology operates by taking apart everything. At the age of 13, I adapted that into sports, balancing basketball with my creative and hungry mind for answers. At age 18, I became passionate about software and how it is impactful in our everyday lives. Since then, I've focused on using my passion for software to help connect people with others, and help individuals build within themselves and their own passions. Software is my map, the catalysts to connectivity.
